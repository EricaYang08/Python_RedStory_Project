With the magnifying glass in hand, Red began to scrutinize every nook and cranny of the cottage. Her keen eyes caught a glimpse of something unusual.

Discoveries:

	Wolf's Footprints: There, on the wooden floor, were faint but distinct wolf footprints leading towards the bedroom door.

	Other Clues: She also noticed some strands of fur caught on the edge of Granny's rocking chair.
